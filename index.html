Research Interests:

My research field focuses on the computational modeling of visual information processing mechanisms, especially of the low- and middle-level of the visual system, aiming to explain some physiological observations, as well as to build some biologically-inspired algorithms for computer vision applications.

  • Computational Mechanisms and Models in The Early Vision.
  • Color Processing in The Visual System.
  • Image Processing and Computer Vision (e.g., Color Constancy, Image Segmentation, Visual Search, etc.).
