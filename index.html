Biography

I am currently a Associate Research Professor at UESTC, China. Before that, I received my Ph.D. degree fromSchool of Life Science and Technology,>University of Electronic Science and Technology of China,in 2016.

Research Interest

My research is aiming at building brain-inspired algorithms for image processing and computer vision applications. To explore the underlying computational theory of visual perception and learning, and to build more powerful machine learning system based on the basic principles of cognitive computing. Recently, I focus on the following research topics:
  • image processing
  • Cognitive Computing
  • Camera pipeline
  • Brain-inspired Computer Vision
